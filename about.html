Kelsey

Kelsey is a senior computer science major at the University of St. Thomas. She transferred after her freshman year from the University of Missouri- Columbia, where she was a computer science major. Kelsey is from the Twin Cities suburbs and went to high school at Thomas Jefferson High School. She is active with the University of St Thomas Swim and Dive team. In her free time she like to run, play softball, and cross country ski. She is looking forward to what the future holds for her in the technology community.
